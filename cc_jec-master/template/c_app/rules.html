Rules and Guidelines:

  • Eligibility: A Curious JECian currently enrolled in any program (UG or PG).
  • Profile Submission Last date-
  • Students need to fill the Hard copy of Registration form and submit it to the Club representative before the last date.
  • Recruitment process:
    • First round-students will be judged on the basis of their profile submissions.
    • Second round-Selected students will be called for Group Discussion.
    • Third round- members will be selected on the basis of a presentation.
  • Further details and dates will be provided on our Facebook Page.
  • Points to remember while filling the profile form:-
    • It shall be filled in English Language only.
    • People with relevant skills and achievements will be given extra credits. These can be technical as well as non technical.
    • Only a single page form will be accepted. It has to be filled in the given space.
    • To the point Answer would be appreciated.
  • In case of any discrepancy, Decision of Club members will be final.
